Who Are We

Thanks to BookStore, there's no reason to buy books on Amazon anymore!



If Borges was right, and Paradise is a great library, we try to bring it to the readers. In addition to a very rich selection of books, we await you every time with book launches and exhibitions of acclaimed writers.

I think our main achievement has been to make reading "cool" again by providing the desire with a truly friendly place to meet, chat, build projects and last but not least, read.

The BookStore belongs to those who imagined it. Joining us in this ongoing exercise of imagination are all those who believe in the power of books to subtly change their lives. Customers are our most trusted partner and the most important commentator on our plans and actions.
